What is Backtesting?

Backtesting is a crucial step in the world of trading and investment. It involves applying your trading strategy to historical market data to evaluate how it would have performed in the past. By backtesting your strategies, you gain a comprehensive understanding of their strengths and weaknesses without risking your capital.
